# ExperQuick Research Infa

**ExperQuick** is an initiative enabling transparent, scalable, and collaborative scientific experimentation. Build, manage, and reproduce research workflows with ease.

## 🌍 Our Mission
To provide easy interface to research and/or experimental mess.

To make researchers and R&D section focus more on decision making rather than managing  codes/ experimental-configs their atifacts etc  all.

---

## 🚀 What We Do
Research often feels like a maze of scattered experiments, disconnected code versions, and inconsistent results.  

ExperQuick brings clarity with integrated workflows, ensuring reproducibility and making collaboration effortless.

### Core Goals:
- 🧩 **Streamline Experiment Workflows** — manage code, data, and results in harmony.  
- 🧠 **Guarantee Reproducibility** — every run is traceable and regenerable.  
- 🤝 **Empower Collaboration** — share results and configurations seamlessly.  
- ⚙️ **Support Any Domain** — from AI and data science to computational chemistry and quantitative analysis.

---

## 🧰 Our Tools
- **[PyLabFlow](https://github.com/ExperQuick/PyLabFlow)**: A lightweight, domain-agnostic framework for managing experiments and workflows.
- **[PyTorchLabFlow](#)**: PyLabFlow with Deep learning plug-in. It is under development but you can access  a limited  version at [BBEK-Anand/PyTorchLabFlow](https://github.com/BBEK-Anand/PyTorchLabFlow)
